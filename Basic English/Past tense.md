
# 1. Past simple

**We use past simple for:**

1. Actions or events in the past
			
	> I went home after the party.

2. Actions or events which happened one after another

	> After I finished my homework, I went to the gym.

3. Things which happened over a long period in the past
	> When I was at the university, I played footbal.


**Examples**
1) *Affermative form:*
    > I **payed** footbal yesterday.
2) *Negative form*
    > I **didn't (did not) play** footbal yesterday.
3) *Interrogative form*
    > **Did** you **played** footbal yesterday?
